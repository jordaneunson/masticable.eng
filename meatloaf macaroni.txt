meatloaf macaroni

put 7$ worth o'proscuitto roll, the rolly one. With the herbs demarcation between meat and fat
extract fat from proscuitto, into a pan and cook low, water and lid help when too hot

add ( beef|veal|pork ) & cook
add wine, wine != prosecco, & fry
add ( carrot|celery|onion|shallot melange ) & fry
garlic & fry then {
	turn up heat to hot
	whole rosemary
	bay leaves
	1 part moms tomato sauce
	1/2 part classico sauce
 	broth
	while ( taste = poor )
		season
	lid
}


wait 30 minutes
boil 1/2 macaroni 1/2 egg noodles
turn on over to 300
al dente, mix to sauce pan
dump into meatloaf pan
shred marble atop
oven till melt
broil till happy
let sit 10 mins
top with {
	mad salt flakes
	finely grated parm
	finely chopped parsley
}
